The Pit Stop Eats of Athens, Ohio

When you need a snack or quick meal in Athens, here are my top recommendations.

The Front Room Coffeehouse

  • 1 Park Pl | 4th Floor
    • With daily afternoon meetings inside Baker University Center, Front Room is very convenient whenever I need a quick-pick-me-up iced tea lemonade or warm hot chocolate in the colder months of the semester.

    West 82

  • 1 Park Pl | 1st Floor
    • If I need more than just something to quench my thirst, the loaded nachos at West 82’s Verde Blanco station are a tasty and filling on-the-go option. If time permits, I’ll grab a bowl and enjoy the beautiful Athens from Front Room’s back outside seating area.

    Fry'd

  • 5 N Court St
    • Ever since the Wendy’s on South Court St closed permanently, Fry’d has been an excellent substitute for an easy-to-walk-with snack on the way home from class. Crispy fries topped with their classic Fry’d sauce that leaves you scraping the bottom of the cone for every last dollop.

    Bubbles

  • 18 S Court St
    • The Front Room Coffeehouse has the local delights, but Bubbles has some more exotic flavors that I often find myself craving. The purple is little daunting at first, but the taro milk tea has an interesting, light, nutty aftertaste that leaves you want to take just one more sip.

    Odyssey Nutrition

  • 9 W Union St
    • A hidden gem amongst chain restaurants and campus cafes, Odyssey offers healthy meal-replacement smoothies in an abundance of flavors, along with a variety of energy teas when just need a little extra boost. The October special - Jack-o-Lantern energy tea, yuummy!
